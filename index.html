01 / about

The short version

I'm a junior data analyst and full-stack developer studying at the National Open University of Nigeria. I design databases, write queries that answer real business questions, and build the interfaces that put that data in front of people who need it.

Here's the part people don't expect: most of this — including this site — is built from an Android phone, using Acode and Termux. No fancy IDE, no dedicated machine. Just a plan, a terminal, and a lot of pushes to git.
